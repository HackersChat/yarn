# Contacting support for this Yarn.social Pod {{ .InstanceName }}

Hi there 👋 Welcome to [Yarn.social](https://yarn.social) 🤗

This pod {{ .InstanceName }} is one of many decentralised instances of a software called
`yarnd` that powers much of the [Yarn.social](https://yarn.social) network. Please refer
to the [About](/about) page for more information.

Unfortunately, due to increased levels of spam we cannot support anonymous supports
requests anyone. Rest assured that if you are reading this page and are of government
or law enforcement we will endeavour to make every attempt to comply with laws and
regulations as appropriate. As such general support can be reached via IRC on the
Libera Chat IRC Network on the #yarn.social channel.

We're sorry for the inconvenience, but [Yarn.social](https://yarn.social) simply
does not have the funds or resources to build sophisticated systems to combat SPAM
nor do we have the time to combat spam ourselves being time poor.

Thank you! 🙏
